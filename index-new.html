I Love Design

I've been an artist my whole life and I've been designing for web and print for more than 14 years.

I'm Down with Dev

I enjoy developing websites using semantic standard HTML(5)/CSS(3), JavasScript & JQuery.

Terence: The Professional

Working as both designer and/or developer in many different settings gives me the "know-how" to work projects from start to finish. I spend a lot of time creating original designs and exploring new programming techniques.

I'm a web developer and multimedia designer offering design services for both web and print mediums. Over the past decade, I've worked with a constant growing client base consisting of corporations, other web developers, design firms, non-profit organizations and retail businesses.

I like to think of myself as an intuitive, creative thinker and problem-solver. My passion is helping others achieve their goals using development and design and the web just happens to be my preferred medium. My business-like approach to creativity and design result in an array of media representation that is specifically meaningful and relevant to my clients and their consumer base.
